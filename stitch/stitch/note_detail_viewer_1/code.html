1. Vector Spaces

A vector space is a set V on which two operations, vector addition and scalar multiplication, are defined. Let u, v, w be vectors in V and c, d be scalars.

Key Axioms

  • Commutativity of addition: u + v = v + u
  • Associativity of addition: u + (v + w) = (u + v) + w
  • Identity element of addition: There exists an element 0 in V.

Understanding the subspace topology is critical for the exam. Ensure you can prove whether a subset is a subspace by checking closure under addition and scalar multiplication.
